how to use this app
1. Open this app with Xcode. You need to use XCode, a special IDE, to emulate the calculator app.
2. Go to the top left of the IDE and search for the play button.
3. Press play and wait several seconds to load and the app will open on the emulated iPhone screen.
2. Press the numbers you'd like to calculator.
3. To use operators in a calculation, select the buttons with the plus, minus, division, subtraction, or multiplication.
